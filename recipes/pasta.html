Lazy Student Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 500g pasta
  • 200g tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 200g tin of green lentils
  • 200g tin of kidney beans in chili sauce
  • Curry powder (to taste)
  • Grated cheese (to serve)

Steps:

  1. Add the tomatoes, lentils, kidney beans, and curry powder to another saucepan and heat on a medium heat until it starts to bubble, lower the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, boil a large pan of water. Add the pasta and cook according to the packet's instructions.
  3. Drain the pasta and serve with grated cheese.
