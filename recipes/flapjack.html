Flapjack

Ingredients:

  • 500g oats
  • 300g golden syrup
  • 200g butter, plus extra for greasing the baking tin
  • Any dried fruit or chocolate chips you want to put in the flapjack

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 160 degrees C. Grease and line baking tin.
  2. Heat the butter and syrup in a saucepan, stirring until melted.
  3. Once the butter has melted, remove pan from the heat and mix in the oats and dried fruit/chocolate chips.
  4. Add the mixture to the tin and bake for 40-50 minutes or until golden brown.
